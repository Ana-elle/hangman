Hangman - Flower version

Rules

  • Choose a level of difficulty.
  • The goal is to guess the word where each letter is represented by a blank space. One letter will be displayed at the beginning to help you get started.
  • Guess your first letter and click on it thanks to the virtual keyboard on the screen.
  • If you guessed correctly, the letter will appear in (all) the blank space(s) it belongs to. If you guessed an incorrect letter, the flower will loose a petal or a leaf.
  • The goal is to guess the word before the flower lost all its petals!
